Welcome to MedAware! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. By using our platform, you consent to the practices outlined in this policy.

Your information is used to provide and improve our services, process transactions, communicate updates, and ensure platform security. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@medaware.com.
